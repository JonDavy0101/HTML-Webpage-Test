Batman Facts

  • While most superheroes have superpowers, Batman does not. He relies on his martial arts skills, detective skills, physical strength, intellect, wealth, technology, and will to face the criminals in Gotham City.
  • First introduced in 1939, Batman developed a fan base that spawned his own comic book in 1940, titled Batman.
  • Batman is second on IGNs Top 100 Comic Book Heroes of All Time (2011). Superman beat him for first place.
  • More interesting facts about Batman

Batman's History

  1. When Bruce Wayne was a child, his parents were murdered in front of him.
  2. This pushed Bruce to vow to fight for justice to keep criminals like the man that murdered his parents off the streets.
  3. Bruce dresses in black and fights as Batman.
